On-boarding Process Flow

From signed offer letter through to fully integrated team member.

HR
Human Resources / Talent Management — Trigger
Receives signed offer letter → initiate background check & payroll setup
Registers personnel into HCP ACS HikCentral Professional Access Control System.
Distributes start date & details to all Focus Departments.
✉
Notification — Parallel Actions Initiated
HR/TM sends starter package & requirements to all Focus Departments simultaneously
HOD/Manager receives: job description & integration plan.
IT/SAP receives: start date, equipment requirements, software & hardware list.
HSSE/OSS receives: start date for PPE preparation.
Admin/FM receives: start date for workspace preparation.
HM
Head of Department / Manager
Presents job description, required software list & team introduction plan
Receives contact info from HR.
Plans onboarding schedule & role training.
IT
Information Technology / SAP — Decision Point
Prepares accounts, credentials, software & hardware
Must confirm equipment readiness before Day 1.
✓ Ready — Laptop/desktop + credentials available for pickup
✗ Not Ready — Flag to HR/Manager immediately
AD
Administration / Facilities Management
Coordinates desk assignment with Hiring Manager
Prepares physical workspace
Ensures workstation is ready & accessible on Day 1.
HS
Health, Safety, Security & Environment / Occupational Safety & Security
Conducts site induction, safety training, issues PPE & CARE Card
Conducts walkabout familiarisation.
Confirms HR/TM has registered personnel in HCP ACS; updates POB/Occupancy records.
HR
Human Resources / Talent Management — BFI Access Card
Issues BFI Access Card after HCP ACS registration is confirmed
Card issuance logged in HCP ACS.
Employee may access site only after induction is complete.
✓
Employee Fully Integrated
New hire is operational — system access activated, workspace ready, induction completed, BFI Access Card issued, team introduced

Off-boarding Process Flow

Secure, risk-controlled exit procedure.
Access revocation is the first priority.

⚠
CRITICAL: Speed is paramount in off-boarding.
Digital access revocation (email, VPN, SaaS tools) must occur on the day of notification — not at end of notice period.
HR
Human Resources / Talent Management — Trigger
Receives notice of departure → triggers entire off-boarding process
HR/TM notifies all Focus Departments immediately.
Distributes exit checklist to all Focus Departments.
IT
Information Technology / SAP — IMMEDIATE ACTION (Day of Notification)
Revokes all digital access: email, VPN, SaaS tools, internal systems
Highest priority action:
Data retrieval & backup must be completed before revocation.
This is a security-critical step — no delays permitted.
EMAIL VPN SAAS TOOLS INTERNAL SYSTEMS
HM
Head of Department / Manager
Drafts handover documentation & knowledge transfer plan
Must liaise with HR/TM on knowledge transfer requirements.
Ensures critical role knowledge is captured before departure.
HS
Health, Safety, Security & Environment / Occupational Safety & Security
Retrieves PPE & deactivates POB/Occupancy records
Confirms personnel deactivation in HCP ACS with HR/TM.
HR
Human Resources / Talent Management — BFI Access Card
Retrieves & deactivates BFI Access Card; logs deactivation in HCP ACS
Must confirm card retrieval before exit closure. HSSE/OSS supports POB/Occupancy update.
AD
Administration / Facilities Management
Manages final equipment collection & workspace clearance
Retrieves company laptop/desktop, peripherals & any assigned assets.
Clears & reassigns workspace.
HR
Human Resources / Talent Management — Closure
Processes final pay, complete exit documentation, conducts exit interview
Confirms all checklist items completed by all Focus Departments before closing the off-boarding record.
✓
Exit Complete
All access revoked, assets recovered, documentation filed, final pay processed

Critical Control Points

High-risk steps where failure causes the greatest security, safety, or compliance exposure.

On-boarding Controls

💻

IT Equipment & Access Readiness Check

Equipment & credentials must be confirmed ready before Day 1.
A Go/No-Go decision must be communicated to HR/TM & HOD/Manager.
Failure causes lost productivity from day one & reputational harm.

IT/SAP Must confirm by: end of day prior to start date
🦺

HSSE Induction Before Commencing Work

Employee must complete site induction, safety training, & receive PPE before being permitted to commence any work activities.
This is a legal & safety compliance requirement.

HSSE Dept Must complete: Day 1 before work begins
🔐

Access Control Registration

Personnel must be registered in the HCP ACS by HR/TM before they are given site access, then issued the BFI Access Card.
HSSE/OSS then tracks the physical access card for POB/Occupancy.

HR/TM → HSSE/OSS

Off-boarding Controls

⚠
The following controls are security-critical.
Delay in any of these creates active data loss or unauthorised access risk.
🚫

Immediate Digital Access Revocation

All digital access — email, VPN, SaaS tools, internal systems — must be revoked on the same day notice of departure is received, not at end of notice period.
Data must be backed up before revocation.

IT/SAP TARGET: Day of notification
🪪

BFI Access Card Retrieval & Deactivation

HR/TM must retrieve & deactivate the site access card upon departure.
This closes the physical security loop.
Card deactivation must be logged in the HCP ACS.

HR/TM → HSSE/OSS Must confirm to HR before exit closure
📋

Knowledge Transfer Documentation

HOD/Manager must ensure all critical role knowledge, active project status, & institutional knowledge is documented during the notice period.
HR/TM must confirm this is complete before processing final pay.

HOD/Manager Must complete during notice period
💼

Company Asset Recovery

Admin/FM must collect company equipment (laptop/desktop, peripherals, ID).
HSSE/OSS must retrieve PPE.
HR/TM may not process final pay until both departments confirm recovery.

Admin / FM + HSSE/OSS Sign-off required for final pay release
