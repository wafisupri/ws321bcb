🚀 Flowchart Idea 1: Onboarding (The Launch)
Goal: To visually map the transition from "New Employee" to "Productive Team Member."
Focus Departments:
⁃ HR (Human Resource) or TM (Talent Management
⁃ HOD (Head of Department) or Manager
⁃ IT Department
⁃ HSSE Department
⁃ Administration Department with Facilities Management.
The Structure (Swimlanes):
1. HR/TM Lane: Handles paperwork, registers personnel into the access control system.
2. HOD/Manager Lane: Owns the role success, training, and integration plan.
3. IT Lane: Manages digital access and hardware.
4. HSSE Lane: Conducts inductions and training, provides PPE, introduces CARE Card & walkabout.
5. Admin/Facilities Lane: Handles physical workspace setup.
Process Flow Overview (Key Hand-offs to Highlight):
• (Start)
• HR receives signed offer → Triggers the process.
• HR initiates background check & payroll setup.
• →(Notification)
• Sends starter packagedetails/equipment requirements to IT.
• HOD/Manager sends job description, necessary software list, and team introduction plan
• → (Action)
• Receives contact info from HR.
• (Coordination Point)
• Admin/Facilities receives start date.
• → Coordinates desk assignment with Hiring Manager.
• IT prepares accounts.
• → Must confirm equipment is ready (Decision Diamond).
• If Yes: The laptop, credentials, and access cards are ready for pickup.
• If No: Flag to HR/Manager immediately.
• HSSE prepares inductions & training.

📉 Flowchart Idea 2: Offboarding (The Exit)
Goal: To map the orderly and secure termination of employment, minimizing risk and ensuring data recovery. Focus Departments:
⁃ HR (Human Resource) or TM (Talent Management
⁃ HOD (Head of Department) or Manager
⁃ IT Department
⁃ HSSE Department
⁃ Administration Department with Facilities Management.
*CRITICAL NOTE: In offboarding, speed is paramount for security. The flowchart should prioritise "Revocation"actions immediately.
The Structure (Swimlanes):
1. HR/TM Lane: Handles the exit process, documentation, and final pay.
2. HOD/Manager Lane: Owns knowledge transfer and handover documentation.
3. IT Lane: Manages digital access revocation and data retrieval.
4. HSSE Lane: Control physical access.
5. Admin/Facilities Lane: Retrieves company property.
Process Flow Overview (Key Handoffs to Highlight):
• (Start)
• HR receives notice of departure
• → Triggers the entire process
• HR notifies all departmental head.
• → (Crucial Hand-off)
• Sends exit checklist and required actions immediately to Focus Departments.
• IT acts first: Immediately revokes access (email, VPN, SaaS tools). This should happen on the day of notification.
• HOD/Manager drafts handover documents.
• → Must liaise with HR for knowledge transfer requirements.
• HSSE manages the access card retrieval.
• Admin/Facilities manages the final equipment collection.
