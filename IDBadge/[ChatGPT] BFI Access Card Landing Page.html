BFI Access Card Management Portal

Employee On‑Boarding & Off‑Boarding Coordination Framework

Corporate Access Governance

This portal outlines the responsibilities, workflow and supporting documentation required for the issuance, management and recovery of BFI Access Cards throughout the employee lifecycle.

Overview

The BFI Access Card serves as the official identification and controlled access credential for employees and authorised personnel.
The process requires collaboration between HR/TM, HOD/Manager, IT/SAP, HSSE, Admin/FM.

  • Card request and approval
  • Access level assignment
  • Card issuance and acknowledgement
  • Asset recovery during separation
  • Access revocation and record retention

On‑Boarding Departments

  • HR/TM: Employee master data and onboarding request. Card production, issuance and access control coordination
  • HOD/Manager: Access requirements validation.
  • IT/SAP: System account provisioning.
  • HSSE: Safety induction, compliance verification, PPE issuance.
  • Admin/FM: Workstation preparation.

Off‑Boarding Departments

  • Recover access card and company assets.
  • Deactivate physical access rights.
  • Disable IT accounts and credentials.
  • Update personnel records.
  • Maintain audit trail and closure documentation.
