Corporate Access Governance

This portal outlines the responsibilities, workflow, and supporting documentation required for the issuance, management, and recovery of BFI Access Cards throughout the employee lifecycle.

The BFI Access Card is the official identification and controlled-access credential for all employees and authorised personnel at Sungai Liang Industrial Park.

Process at a Glance

The BFI Access Card serves as the official identification and controlled-access credential for employees and authorised personnel.

  • All new employees must complete the access card application as part of their on-boarding checklist
  • The Security Team, OSS Section, and HSSE Department are jointly responsible for card issuance and management
  • Access level is assigned based on role and department requirements
  • Access cards must be returned upon termination, resignation, or contract completion
  • Lost or damaged cards must be reported to Security immediately for deactivation and replacement

Employee On‑Boarding Process

Step-by-step procedure for issuing the BFI Access Card to new employees and business partners.

  • 1 — HR Notification
    HR Department notifies Security Team of new employee's start date and details at least 3 business days in advance.
  • 2 — Document Verification
    Employee submits required documents: national ID copy, passport-size photograph, and signed access card application form.
  • 3 — Photo Capture
    Security Team captures employee photograph at the Security Office, SPARK Centre, Ground Floor.
  • 4 — Card Production
    OSS Section processes the access card with employee photo, full name, SAP/BFI number, and department designation.
  • 5 — HSSE Briefing
    HSSE Department conducts safety orientation and briefs the employee on facility access rules and PPE requirements.
  • 6 — Card Handover
    Security Team activates the card and hands it over to the employee. Employee signs the card receipt register.

Employee Off‑Boarding Process

Procedure for access card return and account deactivation when an employee leaves BFI.

  • 1 — HR Notification
    HR notifies Security Team, OSS Section, and HSSE of employee's last working day at least 5 business days in advance.
  • 2 — Clearance Check
    Employee completes department clearance from their section head, confirming no outstanding items or equipment.
  • 3 — Card Return
    Employee returns the physical access card to the Security Office at SPARK Centre. Security verifies and logs card return.
  • 4 — Deactivation
    OSS Section deactivates the card in the access control system within 24 hours of return. HSSE updates safety records.
  • 5 — Final Confirmation
    Security Team issues a clearance certificate confirming card return and system deactivation for exit documentation.
