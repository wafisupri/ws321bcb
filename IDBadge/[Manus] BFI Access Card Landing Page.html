BFI Access Card 2025

Streamlining the employee on-boarding and off-boarding process for Brunei Fertilizer Industries.

Access Card Overview

The BFI Access Card is a mandatory identification and security credential for all employees of Brunei Fertilizer Industries Sdn Bhd. It ensures secure access to the SPARK Centre, Sungai Liang Industrial Park, and all associated facilities.

This card remains the property of BFI and must be presented upon request by the Security Section. It integrates seamlessly with our SAP systems for comprehensive personnel management.

On-boarding Process

The issuance of a new BFI Access Card involves coordination across multiple departments to ensure security and compliance:

  • Human Resources (HR): Initiates the process upon hiring, assigning the SAP No. (BFI No.) and validating employee details.
  • IT Department: Provisions digital access rights and links the SAP No. to internal network systems.
  • HSSE Department: Conducts mandatory safety inductions. An access card will not be activated until safety clearance is granted.
  • Security Team (OSS Section): Responsible for the physical printing of the card, photo capture, and final activation of physical access gates.

Off-boarding Process

To maintain site security, a strict off-boarding protocol is enforced when an employee departs BFI:

  • Human Resources (HR): Notifies all departments of the termination/resignation date.
  • IT Department: Revokes all digital and network access immediately upon the effective date.
  • Security Team (OSS Section): Deactivates the physical access card in the security system. The employee must surrender the physical card to the Security Section on their final day.
  • HSSE Department: Updates the active personnel roster for emergency response purposes.
