Access Card Lifecycle

Onboarding & Offboarding at a Glance

Every department plays a critical role in issuing, managing, and reclaiming BFI Access Cards throughout the employee lifecycle.

👥

Human Resources

Initiates the access card request upon employee confirmation. Provides personal details, SAP number, and employment status to OSS Section.

🔐

Security Team — OSS Section

Primary custodian of the access card process. Produces, activates, and collects cards. Manages access levels and maintains the card registry.

🛡️

HSSE Department

Ensures compliance with safety and security standards. Conducts mandatory safety induction before card activation for site access.

💻

Information Technology

Integrates card credentials with IT systems. Enables logical access to BFI networks, applications, and digital resources.

💰

Finance Department

Handles card-related deposits or charges. Verifies clearance of financial obligations before final offboarding sign-off.

🏢

Administration

Coordinates logistics for card collection and return. Manages employee records and supports inter-departmental communication.

End-to-End Process Flow

1

HR Initiation

Employee confirmed. HR submits access card request form with full details.

2

HSSE Induction

Mandatory safety & security briefing completed before card issuance.

3

OSS Production

Security Team produces, encodes, and activates the access card.

4

IT Integration

Card linked to IT systems for network and application access.

5

Card Issuance

Employee collects card from OSS Section upon clearance.

6

Offboarding Return

Card deactivated and returned to OSS upon employee exit.
