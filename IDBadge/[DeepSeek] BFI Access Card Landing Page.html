Employee Lifecycle Management

Departments involved in both On‑Boarding & Off‑Boarding of employees. Select a tab to explore.

On‑Boarding

Seamless integration of new employees into the BFI ecosystem. Multi‑department coordination ensures compliance, safety, and operational readiness.

  • Access Card issuance & activation
  • Safety induction & HSSE briefing
  • IT system & email account setup
  • PPE issuance & facility orientation
  • Employee record creation (HR)
Security OSS HSSE HR IT

Off‑Boarding

Structured separation process that protects company assets, ensures data security, and maintains a positive employee experience.

  • Access Card revocation & collection
  • IT system & account deactivation
  • PPE return & facility clearance
  • Exit interview & final settlement
  • Employee record archival (HR)
Security OSS HSSE HR IT

Lifecycle ownership: The Security Team is the primary custodian of the Access Card lifecycle, with OSS providing operational support, HSSE ensuring safety compliance, and HR maintaining employee records.
