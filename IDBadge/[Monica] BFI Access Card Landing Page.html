Overview

The BFI Access Card is essential for employees at Brunei Fertilizer Industries. It serves as identification and access to various facilities.

RACI Summary

RACI (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed) summary for departments involved in onboarding and offboarding:

  • HR: Responsible for onboarding processes.
  • IT: Responsible for access permissions.
  • Security: Accountable for card issuance.
  • Management: Consulted during offboarding.
