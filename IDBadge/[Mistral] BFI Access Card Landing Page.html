Overview

The BFI Access Card is a critical component for managing employee access during onboarding and offboarding processes at Brunei Fertilizer Industries. This card ensures secure and efficient access to BFI facilities and systems, streamlining the transition for new hires and departing employees.

The onboarding process involves coordination between the Security Team, OSS Section, and HSSE Department to issue access cards, provide necessary training, and ensure compliance with safety protocols.

For offboarding, the card is deactivated, and access is revoked in a timely manner to maintain security and accountability.
