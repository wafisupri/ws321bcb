On-boarding Process Flow

From signed offer letter through to fully integrated team member.

HR
HR / Talent Management — Trigger
Receive signed offer letter → initiate background check & payroll setup
Registers personnel into Access Control System. Distributes start date & details to all departments.
✉
Notification — Parallel Actions Initiated
HR sends starter package & requirements to all departments simultaneously
IT receives: equipment requirements & software list. HOD receives: job description & integration plan. Admin receives: start date for workspace prep.

Prepare job description, required software list, and team introduction plan
Receives contact info from HR. Plans onboarding schedule and role training.
IT

Prepare accounts, credentials, hardware & access cards
Must confirm equipment readiness before Day 1.
✓ Ready — Laptop + credentials available for pickup ✗ Not Ready — Flag to HR/Manager immediately

Ensures workstation is ready and accessible on Day 1.

Introduce CARE Card system. Conduct walkabout familiarisation. Register employee in physical access system.
✓
Employee Fully Integrated
New hire is operational — system access active, workspace ready, induction complete, team introduced.

Off-boarding Process Flow

Secure, risk-controlled exit procedure. Access revocation is the first priority.

⚠
CRITICAL: Speed is paramount in off-boarding. Digital access revocation (email, VPN, SaaS tools) must occur on the day of notification — not at end of notice period.
HR
HR / Talent Management — Trigger
Receive notice of departure → trigger entire off-boarding process
HR notifies all departmental heads immediately. Distributes exit checklist to all focus departments.
IT
IT Department — IMMEDIATE ACTION (Day of Notification)
Revoke all digital access: email, VPN, SaaS tools, internal systems
Highest priority action. Data retrieval and backup must be completed before revocation. This is a security-critical step — no delays permitted.
EMAIL VPN SAAS TOOLS INTERNAL SYSTEMS
HM
HOD / Manager
Draft handover documentation and knowledge transfer plan
Must liaise with HR on knowledge transfer requirements. Ensures critical role knowledge is captured before departure.
HS
HSSE Department
Manage physical access card retrieval
Deactivate and retrieve site access card, PPE, and CARE Card. Update access control records.
AD
Administration / Facilities
Manage final equipment collection and workspace clearance
Retrieve company laptop, peripherals, and any assigned assets. Clear and reassign workspace.
HR
HR / Talent Management — Closure
Process final pay, complete exit documentation, conduct exit interview
Confirm all checklist items completed by all departments before closing the off-boarding record.
✓
Exit Complete
All access revoked, assets recovered, documentation filed, final pay processed.

Critical Control Points

High-risk steps where failure causes the greatest security, safety, or compliance exposure.

On-boarding Controls

💻

IT Equipment & Access Readiness Check

Equipment, credentials, and access cards must be confirmed ready before Day 1. A Go/No-Go decision must be communicated to HR and Manager. Failure causes lost productivity from day one and reputational harm.

IT Dept Must confirm by: end of day prior to start date
🦺

HSSE Induction Before Commencing Work

Employee must complete site induction, safety training, and receive PPE before being permitted to commence any work activities. This is a legal and safety compliance requirement.

HSSE Dept Must complete: Day 1 before work begins
🔐

Access Control Registration

Personnel must be registered in the physical access control system by HR before they are given site access. HSSE then issues and tracks the physical access card.

HR/TM → HSSE Dept

Off-boarding Controls

⚠
The following controls are security-critical. Delay in any of these creates active data loss or unauthorised access risk.
🚫

Immediate Digital Access Revocation

All digital access — email, VPN, SaaS tools, internal systems — must be revoked on the same day notice of departure is received, not at end of notice period. Data must be backed up before revocation.

IT Dept TARGET: Day of notification
🪪

Physical Access Card Retrieval & Deactivation

HSSE must retrieve and deactivate the site access card upon departure. This closes the physical security loop. Card deactivation must be logged in the access control system.

HSSE Dept Must confirm to HR before exit closure
📋

Knowledge Transfer Documentation

HOD must ensure all critical role knowledge, active project status, and institutional knowledge is documented during the notice period. HR must confirm this is complete before processing final pay.

HOD/Manager Must complete during notice period
💼

Company Asset Recovery

Administration must collect all company assets (laptop, hardware, ID, PPE) before the employee's last day. HR may not process final pay until asset recovery is confirmed by Admin and HSSE.

Admin / Facilities Sign-off required for final pay release
